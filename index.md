 ### babbling
> */bab(É™)liNG/*
>
> *adjective*
> 
> 1. talking rapidly and continuously in a foolish, excited, or incomprehensible way.  
>    *"the noise of babbling tourists"*
> 
> 2. (of flowing water) making a continuous murmuring sound.  
>    *"a gently babbling brook"*
